2017 UtahJS Pay, Education, Job-title Survey

In October 2017 a discussion on UtahJS Slack prompted a survey of developers about pay, career progression, and compensation. Some analysis of the results are provided here, and the raw data is available in this repository.

Commentary

  • The lowest earning university graduate earns about the average of all bootcamp graduates
  • Traditional university graduates have the largest spread, earning very high or low
  • Bootcamp's haven't been around for that long, perhaps all grads are still young in their careers

Salary by Experience & Education Type

Commentary

  • Inexperienced self-taught and bootcamp graduates are the lowest earners, starting between 35-60k
  • No Bootcamp graduate who answered the survey earns >$100k
  • Traditional university graduates on average earn higher than their peers at all experience levels
  • The earnings sweetspot of respondents is between 6-12 years experience with between $120-140k salary
